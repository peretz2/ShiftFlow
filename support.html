Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start tracking a shift?

Tap the "Start Shift" button on the main screen. The timer will begin running, and you can pause/resume or stop the shift at any time. Your shift data is automatically saved.

Can I edit a shift after it's completed?

Yes! Tap on any shift in your history to open the details screen. From there, you can edit the times, breaks, tips, notes, or even delete the shift if needed.

What's the difference between Free and Premium?

The free version includes basic shift tracking and summaries. Premium is a one-time $5 purchase that unlocks advanced features including:

  • Monthly net goal tracking and simulations
  • Income forecasts based on your work patterns
  • Payslip comparison tool
  • Unlimited workplaces
  • Data export (PDF, CSV, Excel)
  • iCloud sync across devices
  • Face ID/Touch ID app lock
  • Advanced settings and expanded knowledge center
How do I add multiple workplaces?

Tap the workplace selector at the top-left of the main screen (Premium feature). You can add multiple workplaces, each with its own wage settings, break rules, and payroll options. Switch between them instantly.

How does iCloud sync work?

If you enable iCloud sync (Premium feature), your shift data is automatically synced across all your iOS devices signed in to the same iCloud account. All data is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud storage.

Is my data secure?

Yes. All your shift and financial data is stored locally on your device with iOS security protections. We do not collect or store your data on our servers. Premium users can enable Face ID/Touch ID for additional app-level security.

How accurate are the calculations and forecasts?

ShiftFlow calculates earnings based on the data you provide. However, you should always verify calculations against your official payslips. Forecasts are estimates based on your historical data and may not reflect future reality.

Important: ShiftFlow is a tracking tool, not financial or legal advice. Always consult with professionals for tax, legal, or employment questions.
Can I export my shift data?

Yes! Premium users can export monthly reports as PDF or CSV/Excel files. This is useful for personal records or sharing with accountants.

What if I lose my data?

If you enable iCloud sync (Premium), your data is automatically backed up. Otherwise, data is only stored locally on your device. We recommend enabling iCloud sync or regularly exporting your data as backup.

How do I restore my Premium purchase?

Go to Settings → Premium → Restore Purchase. This will restore your Premium access on any device using the same Apple ID.

The knowledge center information seems outdated. What should I do?

The knowledge center provides general information about Israeli employment laws and may not reflect the most current regulations. Always verify with official government sources or consult a professional for up-to-date legal or tax information.

Can I use ShiftFlow for multiple jobs?

Yes! Premium users can create unlimited workplaces, each with its own settings. You can easily switch between them and track all your jobs in one place.

Troubleshooting

The app crashed or is not responding

Try force-closing the app and reopening it. If the problem persists, restart your device. If you continue experiencing issues, please contact support with details about what you were doing when the crash occurred.

My shift timer stopped tracking

ShiftFlow is designed to continue tracking even when the app is closed. If the timer stops unexpectedly, please check that you haven't accidentally paused the shift. If this happens repeatedly, contact support.

iCloud sync is not working

Ensure you're signed in to iCloud on all devices, have iCloud Drive enabled, and have sufficient iCloud storage. Try toggling sync off and on in settings. If issues persist, contact support.
